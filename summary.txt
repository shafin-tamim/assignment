Alupa Foods Private Limited, a company based in Udupi, Karnataka, has appointed Mallya & Mallya as its auditor. The appointment was made on April 1, 2022, and was approved at the company's Annual General Meeting (AGM). Mallya & Mallya is a Bangalore-based auditing firm that will be responsible for reviewing the company's financial statements.
